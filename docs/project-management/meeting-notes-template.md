## Quick Tips for Effective Notes

- **Keep it concise:** Focus on decisions and actions, not verbatim conversation
- **Use clear headers:** Make it easy to scan
- **Capture decisions:** Explicitly note what was decided and by whom
- **Track actions:** Every action needs an owner and due date
- **Distribute promptly:** Share notes within 24 hours
- **Link related items:** Reference issues, PRs, or docs when relevant

## Meeting-Type Specific Guidance

### Sprint Planning
- Include sprint goal
- List stories/tasks committed
- Note capacity and velocity
- Capture dependencies and risks

### Stakeholder Sync
- Highlight progress and milestones
- Surface risks and escalations
